How Rewrapped keeps playlists fresh

The app is intentionally simple. It focuses on building the perfect “wrapped” playlists for whenever you want a snapshot of your listening habits.

1. Connect Spotify

Open Rewrapped and sign in with Spotify. We’ll ask for permission to view your top tracks and manage playlists on your behalf.

2. Generate playlists

Choose the timeframes you want—short, medium, long term or all-time. Rewrapped instantly creates playlists with cover art and descriptions.

3. Refresh anytime

Tap refresh in the app or let an automation do it for you. Each update clears the old tracks and fills the playlists with your latest top songs.

Keep everything on autopilot with Shortcuts

Rewrapped supports the Shortcuts app out of the box. Create a personal automation that runs daily (or weekly) to regenerate your playlists without opening the app.

  1. Open the Shortcuts app > Automation.
  2. Create a Personal Automation that runs at your preferred time.
  3. Add the “Run Rewrapped” action and choose “Refresh playlists”.
  4. Turn off “Ask Before Running” so it happens automatically.

Pro tips

  • Schedule it overnight so Spotify is updated by the time you wake up.
  • Use Focus filters or widgets to jump right back into your playlists.
  • Pause the automation any time—your mixes stay right where you left them.
